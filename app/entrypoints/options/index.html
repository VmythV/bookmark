Privacy

Local-first: folder recommendations are computed on-device from your behavior, domains, and keywords — nothing leaves your machine unless you enable a provider.

Embedding: when enabled, bookmark titles/URLs are sent to your configured endpoint for vectorization.

Chat: when enabled, queries are sent to your configured endpoint.

Credentials: API keys are stored locally in plaintext and never uploaded by the extension.
