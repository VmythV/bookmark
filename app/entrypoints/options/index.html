Smart Bookmark — Settings

Privacy

  • Embedding: folder names, bookmark titles, and page text are sent to your configured embedding endpoint only when you enable it. Off by default; with it off, recommendations use only behavior / domain / lexical signal (computed locally, nothing leaves the device).
  • Chat (optional): used in a future iteration for natural-language search. Disabled by default.
  • Credentials: stored locally in plaintext and never uploaded by the extension.
